About

Dear friends,


Welcome to our little plant house. We started from small plant, a tiny heart, with one person. We create this safe space for you to look at plants, listen to musinc, exchange cutting, learn more about plants and educate each others. and talk freely, of course respectfuly. By sharing this contagiously good lifestyle with you, we hope you will do the same for others. Evidence shows that humans thrive in the presence of plants and trees, and plants are even more essential in the concrete jungles of urban centers—so let us help you and your friends bring these little wonders into your home.
